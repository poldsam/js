PR Manager


Job Responsibilities

  • Cultivate mutually beneficial relationships with key reporters, analysts, researchers and other influencers to drive awareness of Edubright, its phenomenal growth and our amazing community of editors.
  • Manage inbound media inquiries, and drive earned media coverage through creative pitches and story angles.
  • Analyze the impact stories have on the business, and report media relations results that have a material impact on our business.
  • Collaborate with Marketing VP and key stakeholders to develop measurable PR programs that support business objectives.
  • Manage speaking and other thought leadership opportunities for Edubright's executives.
  • Manage and build strong rapport with PR agency partners.

About You

  • 5+ years track record crafting, implementing and leading successful public relations campaigns.
  • Strong media contacts, particularly across technology and business beats, and across digital, print and broadcast media.
  • Journalism or agency experience a big plus.
  • Social media savvy, and experienced developing and implementing digital campaigns for PR.
  • Persuasive written and verbal communication skills.
  • Strategic thinker with clear sense of the ingredients needed to secure impactful media coverage.
  • Able to work independently in a fast-paced environment, and ability to effectively manage strong priorities.
