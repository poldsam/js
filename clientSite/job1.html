Content Developer


Job Responsibilities

  • Research, develop, and write content for Edubright’s owned and third-party content channels including the blog, forums and contributed sites.
  • Monitor social conversations and engage on media platforms and verticals on behalf of Edubright.
  • Create content for social channels as well as helping to grow our online community by maintaining Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and other media accounts.
  • Create videos, GIFs, illustrations and other content.
  • Help to develop the messaging of email and marketing campaign copy and other various web materials as needed.
  • Assist in building and maintaining social media calendar and strategy.

About You

  • A true digital native; you know how to live in the online world and you create content constantly.
  • You love social media trends and can’t stay out of a conversation.
  • Proven ability to write clearly, persuasively and with a strong voice.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • A clear understanding of standard digital and social communications media such as YouTube, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat LinkedIn, Reddit, forums and blogs.
  • The ability to effectively prioritize tasks and meet requirements for timeliness and accuracy.

Bonus Qualifications

  • Experience using photo editing, illustration and video editing software
  • Experience with Wordpress, social media software
  • Experience with HTML
