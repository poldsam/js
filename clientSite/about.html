Who We Are?
Founded in 2017, Edubright is a one-stop shop for international applicants seeking help with their applications to top universities in the United States. We know how to make the international experience count. All our editors have been successful at undergraduate or graduate applications to top United States institutions and many have been awarded globally competitive scholarships.

Superior Quality
Our editors are Ivy League alumni with one or more degrees from Ivy League schools and with 4+ years of experience in editing applications to undergraduate and graduate programs in the United States.
